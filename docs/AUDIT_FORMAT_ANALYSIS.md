# Audit Format Analysis: Sample vs Generated

## Key Findings from Sample Audits

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bebas Neue (large, bold, uppercase)
  - Main section titles: ~3.75em - 5.67em
  - Subsection titles: ~1.17em - 2.17em (Montserrat SemiBold)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat (Regular, Light, SemiBold variants)
  - Regular text: ~1.17em
  - Line height: ~1.3em - 1.56em

### Colors
- **Primary Text**: #000000 (black) or #262626 (dark gray)
- **Accent Green**: #65DA4F (matches our current)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (white)
- **Secondary Text**: #717171 (gray)

### Section Structure (from Ritual Hockey sample)
1. **KAV Section**:
   - Large title: "KAV: Your klaviyo total revenue [ytd]"
   - Multiple subsections with detailed analysis
   - Charts with specific color scheme
   - Year-over-year comparisons

2. **List Growth**:
   - Title: "LIST GROWTH"
   - Charts showing monthly data
   - Detailed narrative paragraphs
   - Areas of opportunity tables

3. **Data Capture**:
   - Form performance tables
   - Detailed analysis
   - Multiple subsections

4. **Strategic Recommendations**:
   - "Growing Your KAV" section with:
     - Objectives
     - Bullet points with detailed actions
     - Implementation frameworks
   - "Suggested Next Steps" section

### Key Differences to Address

1. **Font Sizes**: Our section titles (2.5rem) may be smaller than sample
2. **Subsections**: Sample has more detailed subsections per major section
3. **Narrative Depth**: Sample has 5-7 paragraph analyses vs our 1-2 paragraphs
4. **Areas of Opportunity**: Sample has structured 3-column tables
5. **Implementation Details**: Sample includes step-by-step frameworks
