## üìä **SECTION 1: Revenue Attribution & KAV Methodology**

### Critical Questions:
1. **Attribution Model Preference**
   - "When you calculate KAV, do you use single-touch or multi-touch attribution?"
   - "Should flow revenue and campaign revenue be mutually exclusive, or can they overlap?"
   - "How do you handle cases where both a flow and campaign touch the same customer?"

2. **Metric Selection**
   - "Do you prefer 'Ordered Product' or 'Placed Order' for total revenue? Why?"
   - "When the dashboard shows different revenue figures, which source do you trust most?"
   - "How do you handle discrepancies between Klaviyo's dashboard and API data?"

3. **KAV Calculation Edge Cases**
   - "What do you do when attributed revenue exceeds total revenue? (We've seen this happen)"
   - "How do you explain KAV percentages above 50% to clients?"
   - "What's the minimum KAV percentage you consider 'good' for different industries?"

## üîÑ **SECTION 2: Flow Analysis & Optimization**

### Critical Questions:
4. **Flow Performance Standards**
   - "What open/click rates do you consider 'excellent' vs 'needs improvement'?"
   - "How do you prioritize which flows to optimize first?"

5. **Timing & Trigger Analysis**

## üìã **Meeting Preparation Checklist**

Before the meeting:
- [ ] Review recent audit reports (if available)
- [ ] Prepare sample output from our system
- [ ] List specific technical questions about API/data
- [ ] Identify 3-5 highest-priority questions
- [ ] Prepare to show current system capabilities
- [ ] Have examples of edge cases we've encountered

During the meeting:
- [ ] Take detailed notes
- [ ] Ask for specific examples/case studies
- [ ] Request access to sample manual audits (if possible)
- [ ] Get clarification on any ambiguous answers
- [ ] Ask "why" to understand reasoning behind preferences

After the meeting:
- [ ] Document all answers
- [ ] Prioritize action items
- [ ] Update implementation roadmap
- [ ] Share findings with team
- [ ] Schedule follow-up if needed
