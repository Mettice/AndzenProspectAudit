Performance Overview

Kloris is significantly outperforming the fashion accessories industry benchmark with 42.6% KAV versus the 30.0% standard, meaning email marketing is driving $119,072 of the $279,407 total revenue generated during this 90-day period. However, the revenue mix reveals a strategic concern: campaigns are generating 56.2% ($66,942) while automated flows contribute only 43.8% ($52,130). In a healthy email program, flows typically drive 60-70% of attributed revenue because they're triggered by customer behavior and run 24/7 without manual effort. This campaign-heavy split suggests Kloris is leaving significant revenue on the table through underutilized automation. The good news: since KAV already exceeds benchmark, the opportunity isn't about catching up to industry standards—it's about optimizing the revenue mix to reduce manual workload while increasing overall attributed revenue through strategic flow development.
